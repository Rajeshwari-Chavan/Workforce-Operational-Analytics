# Enterprise Workforce Operational Efficiency & 360° Appraisal Analytics

An executive-level, production-grade Power BI dashboard engineered to evaluate workforce productivity, track operational quality control, and map 360-degree appraisal dynamics across a 7,000-employee organizational framework.

## 📊 Dashboard Architecture

The reporting ecosystem is split into two targeted, analytical layers to provide macro operational visibility and micro talent evaluation:

1. **Operational Overview (Page 1):** Focuses on core operational metrics, defect trends (error rates), headcount distributions, and critical soft skill drivers.
2. **Talent Appraisal (Page 2):** Drill-down workspace dedicated to 360-degree appraisal feedback mapping, identifying alignment friction between supervisors/peers, and initiative tracking.

---

## 🚀 Executive Key Insights

### Page 1: Operational Efficiency & Quality Control
* **Performance Skew:** An overwhelming **99.4%** of the organization falls into the Medium (62.1%) or Low (37.3%) performance tiers, leaving an elite subset of just **0.6%** occupying the High performance bracket.
* **Quality & Defect Trends:** A staggering correlation exists between execution quality and performance tiers. Average error rates peak sharply at **~6.9** within the Low performance cohort, but plummet down to an absolute minimum baseline of **~4.7** for High performers.
* **The Collaboration Catalyst:** Granular matrix analysis proves that **Collaboration** is the #1 soft-skill performance driver, spiking to an **8.93 average score** for elite talent.
* **Organizational Baselines:** The comprehensive 7K workforce maintains a highly stable macro baseline, reporting a **74.99 Average Productivity Index** paired with a strong **90% global Task Completion Rate**.

### Page 2: 360-Degree Talent Evaluation & Appraisal
* **Interpersonal Weight over Output Tiers:** Advanced cross-filtering isolates key workforce anomalies—such as employees possessing high individual productivity indices (e.g., **85.99**) who are still labeled as **Low performers** due to low supervisor and peer appraisal ratings dropping below **5.80**.
* **The Team Alignment Matrix:** The custom evaluation scatter cloud successfully flags organizational blindspots, pinpointing high-performing independent contributors experiencing extreme team-level or managerial friction.
* **Execution vs. Initiative Metrics:** Employee lifecycle tracking reveals that macro team execution schedules and individual deadline adherence scores heavily fluctuate based on foundational core initiative ratings.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Advanced Implementation
* **BI Platform:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Resolved granular row-level data aggregation conflicts by structuring direct `Employee_ID` relationships to map dense individual scatter clouds without breaking organizational weights.
* **UX/UI Theme:** Developed a bespoke, modern dark-mode template (`#13111C` Canvas / `#1C1A26` Components) featuring neon signal highlights (Cyber Green, Warm Yellow, and Electric Pink) to enforce visual scannability and high accessibility.
